Government Schemes for Women in India

Popular Government Schemes

Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana (PMUY)

PMUY aims to provide LPG connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households to ensure clean cooking fuel.

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Yojana

This scheme focuses on addressing the declining child sex ratio, ensuring education for the girl child and empowering women.
